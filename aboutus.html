About Tantrafiesta

TantraFiesta is the annual national level technical fest​ organized by IIIT Nagpur.​ The mission of Tantrafiesta is to develop competent and capable youth imbued with the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship with the social and environmental orientation to meet the knowledge needs of the country and provide global leadership in Information Technology and allied fields, committing to our motto our event TantraFiesta would showcase the talent in the fields of robotics, algorithm, science, technology, innovation, etc. It is a platform to encourage healthy competition among the budding engineers of our country.
